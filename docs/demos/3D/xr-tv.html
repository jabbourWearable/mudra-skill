Welcome to Mudra Band

📺 XR TV

A floating television you flick through with your wrist — change the channel with a flick of your hand, adjust the volume sideways, and tap to power it on or off.

The app starts in Manual mode so you can practice with on-screen buttons and the keyboard. When your Mudra Band is paired, click Mudra in the top bar to drive the TV from your wrist instead.

Controls

Three gestures drive this app. Each one is also wired to a keyboard shortcut and a button in the simulator pill bar at the bottom.

  • Power on/off: pinch (tap) on your band — keyboard: Space — sim panel: Tap.
  • Change channel: swipe ↑ / ↓ with your wrist — keyboard: ↑ / ↓ — sim panel: ↑ / ↓.
  • Adjust volume: swipe ← / → with your wrist — keyboard: ← / → — sim panel: ← / →.

Practice without a band

You don't need a paired Mudra Band to try this app. While you're in Manual mode, every control above is available from your keyboard and the simulator pill bar at the bottom of the screen.

  • Simulator panel: the pill bar at the bottom of the screen has one button per action — click Tap, ↑, ↓, ←, → to fire the matching Mudra signal.
  • Keyboard: Space taps, ↑ / ↓ change channel, ← / → change volume. While this dialog is open, Page Up / Page Down step through it and Esc closes it at any time.
  • Switch to Mudra mode: when your band is paired, click Mudra in the top bar. The simulator greys out and your wrist takes over.
