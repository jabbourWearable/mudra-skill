Welcome to Mudra Band

🎨 3D Color Picker

Move a cursor over a floating color palette, then hold a button to apply the highlighted hue to the pedestal sphere in front of you.

The app starts in Manual mode so you can practice with on-screen buttons and the keyboard. When your Mudra Band is paired, click Mudra in the top bar to drive the cursor from your wrist instead.

Controls

Two signals drive this app. Each is wired to your wrist, your keyboard, and the simulator pill bar at the bottom of the screen.

  • Move the cursor: continuous wrist motion (navigation) — keyboard: I / J / K / L (up / left / down / right) — sim panel: the four arrow buttons.
  • Pick a color: press and hold the button on your band — keyboard: hold Shift — sim panel: press and hold Select. Release to apply.

Practice without a band

You don't need a paired Mudra Band to try this app. While you're in Manual mode every control above is available from your keyboard and the simulator pill bar at the bottom of the screen.

  • Simulator panel: the four arrow buttons fire navigation deltas (one click ≈ a gentle wrist nudge); the Select button must be pressed and held to mimic a sustained button hold.
  • Keyboard: I / J / K / L move the cursor, hold Shift to pick. While this dialog is open, Page Up / Page Down step through it and Esc closes it at any time.
  • Switch to Mudra mode: when your band is paired, click Mudra in the top bar. The simulator greys out and your wrist takes over.
