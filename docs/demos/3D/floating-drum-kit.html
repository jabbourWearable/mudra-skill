🥁 Floating Drum Kit

A floating five-piece kit you play with your Mudra Band — pinches hit drums in sequence, double-tap crashes the cymbal, twist swaps kit themes.

Pinch your Mudra Band (or press Space) to strike the highlighted drum. Double-tap (Shift+D) for a crash. Twist (T) to switch between Rock, Synth and Jazz.
